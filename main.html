It's Also Killing Animals (This Made Me Really Sad)

Baby Sea Turtles

Over 100,000 baby turtles die every year just in Florida because they get confused by city lights and go the wrong way instead of towards the ocean. This is so sad.

Birds

Millions of birds crash into buildings or get lost during migration because they follow bright lights instead of stars. They literally fly around lights until they die from being too tired.

My Solution: Tell Everyone About This!

I think the main problem is that most people don't even know light pollution is a thing. People will take action if they know about it, maybe they'll make their cities change the lights.

Why I Care About This

I did not grow up in the USA, I grew up in Guatemala, the night sky was beautiful. Now I live in NY and kids here will never get to experience the night sky without pollution.

New York (where I live now)

This is what the night sky looks like in NYC. You can barely see any stars because of all the light pollution. It's pretty depressing.

Guatemala (where I grew up)

This is what I remember from Guatemala - SO many stars! You could see the Milky Way and everything. We didn't even need flashlights because the starlight was bright enough. I want kids everywhere to be able to see this.

The difference is incredible. In Guatemala, nights were magical, you felt connected to the universe. In NYC, you forget there's even a sky above all the buildings. We're stealing this experience from future generations.
